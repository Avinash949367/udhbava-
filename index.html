About Us

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

Udhbhava Hospital is a trusted private multispecialty hospital located in the heart of Banashankari, Bengaluru. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive, patient-first healthcare across a wide spectrum of medical disciplines — from obstetrics and maternity to pediatrics, general medicine, surgery, and emergency care.

Our mission is simple: deliver world-class healthcare with warmth, precision, and accessibility. Operating round the clock, 365 days a year, we ensure that expert medical help is always within reach for families in Ittamadu, Banashankari and beyond.
